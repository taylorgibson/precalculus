We will use \(D_1\) to represent the amount of ibuprofen left after four hours. The subscripts are used to count the steps, or iterations, in the iterative process. In this problem the subscript represents the number of four-hour time periods since Joan took the tablet. In four hours, her kidneys have filtered out \(67\%\) of the drug from her bloodstream, so we write \begin{gather*} D_1=D_0-.67D_0=200-.67\cdot 200=66 \end{gather*}
