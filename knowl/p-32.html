When making monthly payments to repay a loan, an interest payment is charged on all of the money that is owed at the end of each month. In this example, at the end of the first month you will owe \((0.0045)(10,000)=\$45.00\) in interest. After making the first payment you will owe \(\$10,000+\$45-\$230=\$9815\text{.}\).
