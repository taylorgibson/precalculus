Exercise2

Maisha opens a retirement account on her \(35^{th}\) birthday with a deposit of \(\$2,400\text{.}\) Each year on her birthday, she plans to deposit an additional \(\$2,400\text{.}\) The account earns interest at a rate of \(10\%\) annually.

  1. How much will Maisha have saved by the time she retires at age \(65\text{?}\)
  2. Suppose Maisha wants to have \(\$1\) million in the account by age \(65\text{.}\) To the nearest hundred dollars, how much should she deposit each year?
  3. Suppose Maisha starts saving ten years earlier, at age \(25\text{.}\) To the nearest hundred dollars, how much should she deposit each year to have \(\$1\) million at retirement?
